Perks of Being a Wallflower

I really love the main character. He can be relatable to me because I'm introverted and I was very shy in high school as well. And like Charlie, my friends have been a safe space for me. The soundtrack is also really good. I also love the narration in the movie.

Stand By Me

This movie is very special to me. I love Stephen King movies. It was funny, it was sad, and happy all throughout the movie, just like life. It was very enjoyable to me. I wish I could be a 12-year-old boy sometimes. I feel like society makes girls grow up too soon.

Dead Poets Society

I love this movie so much. I just love movies about friendships so much, especially healthy male friendships. I find that society tends to bring down healthy male friendships by telling them that boys can’t compliment another boy because it's “gay” or “feminine”. I found this movie did a great job of portraying healthy male friendships and just youths in general. I love the over all message.
